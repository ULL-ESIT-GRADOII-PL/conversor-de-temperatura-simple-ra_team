Temperature Converter

Enter Temperature:


• Usage of this Temperature Converter:

To use the temperature converter you just need to write the source temperature to be converted and then press 'Enter' (carriage return) and the converted temperature'll be displayed.


• Types of conversions:

1. From Celsius to Farenheit and Kelvin
2. From Farenheit to Celsius and Kelvin
3. From Kelvin to Celsius and Farenheit


• Input Examples:

1. 32F -> Will convert 32 Farenheit to Celsius and Kelvin
2. 45C -> Will convert 45 Celsius to Farenheit and Kelvin
3. 297K -> Will convert 200 Kelvin to Celsius and Farenheit
4. Also you can use exponential notation (E), for example: 1E3f -> Will convert 100 Farenheit to Celsius and Kelvin
5. You can write any substring of Farenheit, Celsius or Kelvin. Also you can write f or F, c or C, k or K... indistinctly.
